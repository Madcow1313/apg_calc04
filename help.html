Help info for my_awesome_calculator!


1. Use digits and dot to enter numbers (up to 255 symbols).
2. Use +,-,*,/ to perform simple mathematical operations.
3. +x and -x stand for unary plus and unary minus accordingly.
4. Use +x and -x because they are different from + and - .
For example: 49 / (unary_minus 7) will give us -7,
49 / (-7) will give us undefined behaviour dew to implementation.
5. Use brackets otherwise get error.
6. Use sin, cos, e.t.c to perform trigonometrical operations.
7. log - is logarithm with base 10, ln - with base e.
8. sqrt stands for square root, ^ - for power.
9. Have fun clicking buttons, because there is no keyboard interaction.:)
